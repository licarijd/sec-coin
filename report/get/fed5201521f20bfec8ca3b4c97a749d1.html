    Description

    2015 Jaguar XJ

    This vehicle will come with a PA Reconstructed Title.

    Please check with the DMV in your state for registration requirements.

    As you can see from the 40+ photos that we have listed this is an awesome looking and very well equipped 2015 Jaguar XJ Supercharged sedan.

    Just to highlight, it features a 3.0L Supercharged V6 motor, 8-speed automatic transmission, Navigation system w/rear view camera, front & rear parking sensors, Xenon headlights, keyless start, full power options including 14-way dual power front seats with dual memory, wood interior trim, leather interior, heated & cooled front and rear seats, heated steering wheel, massage function front seats, quad zone climate control, panorama power roof, Meridian sound system, cd/satellite radio ready audio system, Bluetooth, 19" polished alloy wheels and the list goes on and on.

    The body is in all around great condition.

    The interior is in equally all around great condition, everything works as it should and it is clean.

    Mechanically this Jaguar is in great condition with nothing negative to report. Just push the button, drive and enjoy.

    What you can not see in the photos is that this XJ was previously involved in an accident. The rear bumper assembly, trunklid, both taillights and rear body panel were replaced and the trunk floor was repaired. There was no damage to the front and the airbags did not deploy. It has been professionally repaired by our facility, inspected and titled in PA and comes with a PA Reconstructed Title.

    In a nutshell what you have here is a very well equipped Jaguar XJ sedan that needs nothing more than a new home. It is being discounted strictly for its history.

    ** Please note that the mileage will be slightly higher at time of delivery due to local driving.

      About Us

       Welcome to Feretti Motors  - We are a pioneer in the internet-based pre-owned automotive sales industry and one of the largest dealers of reconstructed and salvage titled vehicles in the United States. With over a decade of combined experience, we offer unbeatable prices, unparalleled customer service, and an unquestionable commitment to make every customer a customer for life.

      Do you want to own the vehicle you've always dreamed of but couldn't quite afford? Thanks to Feretti Motors you can. We specialize in high quality late model pre-owned branded title vehicles. Such titles include Reconstructed, Flood, Recovered Theft, and Salvage Titles. Our inventory features both damaged vehicles with salvage titles that need to be repaired and vehicles that we have already repaired at our facility which now come with Reconstructed, Flood, or Recovered Theft branded titles. All vehicles that we repair pass through a reconstruction inspection by a state certified enhanced inspection mechanic and are ready to be driven and enjoyed with no further repairs or servicing needed.

      From time to time our inventory will also contain damaged or previously damaged vehicles which still retain their clean titles. We always disclose if a vehicle has had prior accident damage even if it has been repaired and still has a clean title. That is the major difference between Feretti Motors and many of our competitors. If a vehicle's price seems to good to be true, then there is most likely a reason for it.

We assume no responsibility for damages incurred after leaving our premises. If the vehicle is going to be shipped, full payment must be received before the vehicle is delivered to the shipping company.

      Buyer's Inspection
